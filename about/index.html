Hey there! 👋

I’m Dinis Peixoto, a software engineer based in Braga, Portugal. 🇵🇹 I have been working at FARFETCH (an e-commerce luxury platform 👠) for over 2 years now.

I consider myself an eager to learn person, always with a learn-by-doing philosophy in mind. Other than that, I really like to feel challenged as it enables me to not only learn new things but also improve my problem solving skills.

I truly believe that teaching is one of the best ways to learn, that’s why I’m currently mentoring csharp and python at exercism.io, after several years of teaching computer science-related subjects to young people at Coderdojo.

The challenges that come with designing, building and scaling distributed systems is something that fascinates me. Moreover, most recently, I have been keen to learn and work towards a machine learning engineer role.

Last but not least, I’m a formula 1 🏎 and skateboarding 🛹 enthusiast, who loves going to the gym every weekday 🏋️‍♂️.
